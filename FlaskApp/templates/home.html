Brain Tumor Recognition using Machine Learning

About

The application accepts MRI images of the brain of a patient and shows a RI map of the brain. The RI map helps the doctors to identify the brain lesions where there is a chance of brain cancer. With the help of the RI values of brain tissues and fluids in theses regions the doctors can conclude whether the patient is a cancer suspect. It can also help to identify the cancer stage by providing the RI values in these regions and size of the mass.
